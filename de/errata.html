Errata

Updated Dec. 20th, 2022

Button Shy Games and the designers of Wildtails appreciate your support and apologize for the following issues or misprints.

Potential Component Issue

There may be potential problem on one of the components that is in the secret sleeve in Wildtails, as well as in the Wildtails Refresh Pack. This has only impacted about 1/8 of the early production run that was sent out and has been corrected on all orders after the issue was caught.

The issue will be described below so if you don’t want very light spoilers, please have someone else read on.

Inside of the secret sleeve are two sticker sheets. They are die cut so that you can peel the stickers, but a small number are drastically miscut and unusable. Please take a look at the cut lines and make sure they are where they are supposed to be. Images of how they should look are linked below so if you want to avoid any spoilers, please have someone else check. After you are done, please make sure you put the components back in the sleeve in the same order they were in.

Sticker Sheet 1

Sticker Sheet 2
