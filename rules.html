Campaign Rules

These special rules apply to the legacy campaign only. For the full game rules, please refer to the Rulebook.

Scenario Setup

Some Chapters have scenarios. These have a setup section which lists the Objective and Threat Card numbers to be used for that particular scenario, as well as a number of scene cards the Threat will capture from the top of the Scene Deck at the start of the game.

Campaign Progress

If a chapter includes a scenario, you must win that scenario in order to complete that chapter and progress. Once you win the scenario, fill in the circle that matches the completed scenario on the Campaign Log card. If you fail a scenario, fill in one of the squares corresponding to the current chapter on the Defeats tracker of the Campaign Log. Then, you may replay that scenario again. If you have filled in all 3 defeats corresponding to the current set of scenarios then you no longer track defeats for that set of scenarios. You still must win the current scenario to progress.

Difficulty Reduction

If you have logged 3 defeats corresponding to the current set of scenarios, for the remainder of those scenarios, during setup you may choose to reduce the starting captured cards to 0.

Chapter Unlocks

Once you complete a chapter you gain its Chapter Unlocks. If a chapter does not have a scenario, any Chapter Unlocks are gained as soon as you finish reading it.

New cards, stickers, and rules may be unlocked as a reward. Whenever anything is unlocked, if it has a symbol, fill it in to show that it is now unlocked and available.
