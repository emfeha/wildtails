Chapter 3

Golden Opportunity

Your smuggling operation in Ratuga Bay is beginning to attract attention from powerful forces. You receive a message from Marcus Gladwell a wealthy American entrepreneur: “A Spanish ship loaded with priceless cargo is passing through the Gulf. Take one of my ships and secure its bounty for me. In exchange, I will offer my protection and assistance when needed.” With a proper patron, you could invest in a full fleet and be able to better counter your enemies. Yet rumors circulate amongst your crew that the Gulf is being heavily patrolled by the U.S. Navy’s most vicious officer: Commodore Gnaw.
