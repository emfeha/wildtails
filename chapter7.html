Pierre’s Wrath

Trouble brews in Ratuga Bay. Still holding a grudge over the captured ship, Pierre Lafayette has gathered a force of mercenaries and raiders and they are sailing towards you. A battle is inevitable, but you have a secret weapon: through your American patron, you hear that one of Pierre’s freshly-hired captains has been complaining about their miserable pay. With a small bribe and a letter of support, you may be able to recruit their services during the fight.

Homeland Calls

The fight is over. Your crews search the wreckage and find no sign of Pierre. Whether he escaped or not, it’s only a matter of time before Jean knows what’s happened. Worse, your American partner has sent word that the British attack is imminent. Their message urges you to think on the profitability that working together will have once the British are defeated. It concludes with a call to defend your homeland, regardless of your current petty squabbles with the Lafayettes.
