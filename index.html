In the early 19th century, a bustling outpost of seafaring vermin thrived in Ratuga Bay, just off the coast of Louisiana. In this campaign you will command a growing operation of smugglers-turned-pirates, build an empire from the ground up, and secure your legacy as a legend among pirates.

Campaign Overview

This Campaign Book contains each chapter of the campaign’s story, as well as campaign-specific rules. Read the Campaign Rules section first, then read the Legacy Campaign’s Prologue: 1983. After that, read each new chapter only after it’s been unlocked.
