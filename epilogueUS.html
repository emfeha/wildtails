Wildtails: A Pirate Legacy
Epilogue

American Folk Heroes

The British have been repelled, and for now, New L’Oreans is safe. For your support, you receive an official Letter of Marque from Louisiana's governor and may now operate as privateers in order to legally plunder foreign ships and weaken America's enemies. This work is quite profitable, and soon your empire is thriving even with Jean Lafayette still scheming against you from the shadows. While you skirmish from time to time with vengeful Commodore Gnaw and other former enemies, you’ve reached the kind of financial success you dreamed about when you first started.
