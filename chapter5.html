Chapter 5

Gnaw's Forces

It only takes one match to light the powderkeg. A British attack has decimated a squadron of American ships, causing Commodore Gnaw to escalate the US presence. The American Forces under Gnaw aggressively patrol the entire Gulf and many pirate crews have gone to ground rather than contend with them.

Yet something promising lies on the horizon. Your scouts report that a single US ship survived the attack and is carrying its valuable cargo of munitions south along the coast. You immediately send your crew after it, before it can get away. Only afterwards do you hear that an unidentified pirate ship is also closing in on your target…
