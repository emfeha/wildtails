Through the Blockade

The British Navy has ordered its strongest ships to form a blockade, preventing American fleets from operating freely. Across the Gulf, Cartalena claims its independence from Spain and opens its port to privateers. To recruit the best sailors and build new contacts, you’ll need to force your way through the waiting British warships. With the letters of your ship’s new name not yet dry, you take a deep breath and set your course for Cartalena.

Celebrate Success

The tavern erupts with laughter and song as Jean concludes a dazzling story. He turns to you and says, “Thank you for a proper pirate’s welcome. I can see you are enjoying your success.” He glances around, and you notice for the first time more than a few suspicious characters watching you. “Newfound success can bring new enemies,” he says with a steely smile. “And you appear to have made more than enough already.” Jean nods politely to the assorted ruffians as he makes his way to the door.

Need help?

Being a successful pirate is difficult. Growing a pirate empire is even harder. Here are some helpful tips to consider.

Be Efficient: When spending resources, try to not waste any that are grouped together if possible. REMEMBER! If either player spends a resource from a card, any unspent resources on that card may still be used until the end of the turn.

Don’t Hoard Resources: Hoarding too many Resource cards that you can’t use will be a quick way to lose the game. The more Resource cards you hold onto, the fewer will get reshuffled back into the deck, which in turn makes for shorter rounds.

Achieve Objectives Quickly: Focus as a team to achieve Objective milestones as soon as possible. Objective rewards can be very powerful and give you more options early on.
